1. Data We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Tessera stores game progress, settings, and preferences locally on your device using localStorage. This data does not leave your device unless you choose to export it.

  • Game progress, completed levels, stars, and streaks
  • Statistics such as best times and total puzzles solved
  • Settings such as sound, haptics, color-blind mode, and themes
  • Saved puzzle state and anonymous local analytics events

We do not operate a server-side user database and we do not sell your game progress data.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

Google AdMob and Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect device identifiers, IP address, general location, device information, and app interaction data for advertising, measurement, and fraud prevention.

Where required by law, the app shows Google's User Messaging Platform consent form before requesting ads. You can reopen ad privacy choices from Tessera Settings when they are available in your region.

Premium users do not see advertisements. The AdMob SDK may still collect limited device data for fraud prevention and analytics in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Platform Store Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through the platform store: Apple StoreKit on iOS, and Google Play Billing on Android when Android is available. We do not use a third-party payment processor, and we do not receive or store your card, Apple ID, or Google payment details.

Leaderboards Deferred for Launch

The current launch build does not enable Apple Game Center, Google Play Games Services, or online leaderboard submissions. If platform leaderboards are enabled in a future version, this policy will be updated before collecting or sharing leaderboard data.

Local Notifications

With your permission, the app may schedule local reminders on your device to help maintain a daily streak. Notification data is not sent to any server.

2. How We Use Data

  • Local game data: used to save your progress and preferences on your device.
  • Advertising data: used by Google AdMob to show ads and measure performance.
  • Purchase data: processed by the platform store; we only store Premium status locally.
  • Platform leaderboard data: not collected in the current launch build.
  • Notification permissions: used only for on-device local reminders.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information ourselves, and we do not run a server-side user database. The app shares data with Google AdMob for advertising, consent, measurement, and fraud prevention, and with the platform store for purchases. The current launch build does not share platform leaderboard data.

4. Children's Privacy

Tessera is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require an account or login to play.

5. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the app presents Apple's standard App Tracking Transparency prompt before any tracking can occur. You may decline tracking and continue using every feature of the app.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All local game data can be deleted at any time by using the Reset All Progress button in the app's Settings screen or by uninstalling the app.

7. Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Delete local game data from Tessera Settings.
  • Request information about third-party service data directly from Apple or Google.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The Last updated date at the top of the page reflects the latest version.
