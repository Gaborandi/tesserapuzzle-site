Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Tessera ("we", "our", or "the app") is a puzzle game developed for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Tessera stores all game progress, settings, and preferences locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it. This includes:

  • Game progress (levels completed, stars earned, streaks)
  • Statistics (best times, total puzzles solved)
  • Settings (sound, haptics, color-blind mode, theme preferences)
  • Saved game state (in-progress puzzles)
  • Anonymous analytics events (gameplay actions such as puzzle starts and completions, stored locally for future service improvement)

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this data on our servers.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • IP address
  • General location (city/region level)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

AdMob uses this data to serve relevant advertisements. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Premium subscribers do not see advertisements. The AdMob SDK may still collect limited device data for fraud detection and analytics in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

In-app purchases are processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not use any third-party payment processor. Purchase transactions are handled by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your payment information (credit card, Apple ID, etc.).

Apple Game Center (Optional Leaderboards)

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, the app reads your Game Center display name and player identifier from Apple in order to submit your daily and journey scores to public leaderboards. Game Center sign-in is optional — you can decline the prompt and continue playing the entire game without it. All Game Center data is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy; we never receive or store your Apple ID, password, or contact details.

Local Notifications (Streak Reminders)

With your permission, the app may schedule local push notifications on your device to remind you to maintain your daily streak. These notifications are generated entirely on-device using Apple's UserNotifications framework — no notification data is transmitted to any server. You can disable notifications at any time via iOS Settings > Notifications > Tessera, or decline the initial permission prompt.

2. How We Use Data

  • Local game data: Used solely to save your game progress and preferences on your device.
  • Advertising data: Used by Google AdMob to display relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • Purchase data: Processed by Apple's StoreKit. We only store your premium status locally on your device.
  • Game Center data: Used only to display your name and rank on Tessera leaderboards. Optional and revocable at any time.
  • Notification permissions: Used only to schedule on-device streak reminders. Optional and revocable at any time.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only data sharing occurs through Google AdMob for advertising purposes, which operates under its own privacy policy. In-app purchases are processed by Apple's StoreKit.

4. Children's Privacy

Tessera is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to play.

5. App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, the app presents Apple's standard App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before any tracking can occur. The prompt is shown only after you have completed at least two puzzles, so you can evaluate the app before deciding. You may decline tracking and continue using every feature of the app — declining only limits the personalisation of advertisements. You can change this permission at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All local game data can be deleted at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Progress" button in the app's Settings screen
  • Uninstalling the app (all localStorage data is removed)

Data collected by AdMob is retained according to Google's privacy policy. Apple handles purchase data retention per Apple's privacy policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Delete all local game data via the app's Settings screen
  • Request information about data collected by third-party services by contacting them directly

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
