FAQ

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Premium section > tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore your Premium status on any device signed into the same Apple ID.

How do I back up my progress?

Go to Settings > Data section > tap "Export Save Data". Copy the text and save it somewhere safe. To restore, go to Settings > "Import Save Data" and paste it back.

I lost my progress after an update.

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you previously exported your save data, you can import it to restore your progress. Unfortunately, if no backup was made, progress cannot be recovered.

How do I remove ads?

Purchase Tessera Premium ($5.99) from the Premium section in Settings. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all advertisements.

What are the three puzzle types?

Logic: Draw territories where no number repeats. Chroma: Mix primary colors to match target shades. Equate: Group numbers that combine via math operations to hit a target.
