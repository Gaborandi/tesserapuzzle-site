Local save data

Progress, settings, and statistics stay on your device unless you export them.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Premium section > tap Restore Purchases. This restores Premium on devices signed into the same platform store account.

How do I back up my progress?

Go to Settings > Data section > tap Export Save Data. Save the text somewhere safe. To restore, go to Import Save Data and paste it back.

I lost my progress after an update.

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you previously exported save data, you can import it. Without a backup, progress cannot be recovered.

How do I remove ads?

Purchase Tessera Premium from the Premium section in Settings. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes advertisements.

What are the three puzzle types?

Logic: draw territories where no number repeats. Chroma: mix colors to match a target. Equate: group numbers with operations to hit a value.
